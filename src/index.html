Practice with Americans for Free!

What is CEC?

CEC is a group of friends gathering every week to enjoy speaking English and intercultural communication between Chinese students and American volunteers!
It is free!

When and Where?

CEC meets every Wednesday night at 18:30 at the track and field of China Agricultural University!

CEC also meets every Friday night at 18:30 at the track and field of Beijing Forestry University!
